The vision
behind the work

The work that I intend to do is one that will serve to help brand owners - those who are truly serious about their brand - to establish something that has value.

Anyone can build a brand, but only some focus on value rather than quick money, money is a by product, value lasts, I will help you to add value to your brand.

I have a skill, perhaps a gift, a skill is something you practice, a gift is something your born with, you've probably heard the quote "Jack of all trades, master of none", but the real undiluted version finishes it with "but often times better than a master of one".

I can't sit long enough to learn one thing, but my heart is set on making clothes, the rest is just a by product to get to the finished product, but I have creative mind and therefore I will act as a creative director for your personal brand.

I will help you conceptualize design ideas that fits your brand, and point you in the direction you should go and how you should go about it, the whole nine-yards.

Kees J R Vieira
